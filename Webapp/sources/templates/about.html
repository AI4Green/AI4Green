About AI4Green

Empowering scientists to make sustainable choices

AI4Green is a suite of software that helps chemists manage their research data, work more efficiently, securely collaborate around the world, and be more sustainable.

Mission Statement

AI4Green is AI4Green is open-source software developed in the group of Jonathan Hirst at University of Nottingham, UK. This work is supported by a grant from the Royal Academy of Engineering.

AI4Green ELN

AI4Green represents a significant advancement in laboratory management systems, combining artificial intelligence with green chemistry principles to create a more sustainable future for scientific research.

Our platform provides researchers with powerful tools to:

  • ✓ Track and optimize reaction conditions
  • ✓ Minimize environmental impact
  • ✓ Collaborate seamlessly with colleagues
  • ✓ Make data-driven decisions

Sustainability Tools

Green Metrics Calculator

Calculate and optimize reaction metrics including atom economy, E-factor, and process mass intensity.

Our calculator helps researchers make informed decisions about their reaction conditions, leading to more sustainable outcomes and reduced environmental impact.

Solvent Selection Guide

AI-powered recommendations for greener solvent alternatives based on your specific reaction conditions.

Make smarter choices about solvents with our intelligent system that considers both effectiveness and environmental impact.

Waste Reduction Analyzer

Track and analyze laboratory waste generation with intelligent suggestions for reduction strategies.

Monitor your lab's environmental impact and receive actionable insights to reduce waste and improve sustainability practices.

Related Software

AI4Green4Students

AI4Green4Students is a specialized version of our platform designed specifically for educational environments. It provides students with hands-on experience in green chemistry principles while learning laboratory skills. The platform includes:

  • • Simplified interface for educational settings
  • • Built-in tutorials and learning resources
  • • Teacher management tools and assignment features
  • • Focus on fundamental green chemistry concepts
